ABOUT

The OPUS MAGNUM STUDIOS provide you with musical solutions for your band, project, within any styles of music the world can offer.

We propose a wide array of services - from mixing and mastering to arrangements and music coaching - which are a definite help for your own musical endeavours.

We aim for results that please you and only you. We are always considerate of emotional content. It is a priority in our work. We go with the music, for the music.

No big-headed decision-making producer, no templates, no compromises.
We craft your desires using our tools, knowledge and professional opinions.
We create your sound for your band, we help you as you want, how much you want.

You are the master of it all ; are we but willing tools.

SERVICES

RECORDING

High quality recordings with the right gear for the right purpose, including percussion, vocals, guitars*, bass*, all kinds of keyboards and more.

* D.I. only for now

MIXING

Mixing your single, EP, album WITH YOU and FOR YOU without compromises : the studio is a tool for your sound.

Your music. Our quality.

Our advice. Your choice.

You envision. We work.

MASTERING

We master any mixes (ours, yours, someone else's) in professional outputs which can be for CDs, vinyl but as well for all nowadays' needs as in streaming, Youtube, Spotify, Bandcamp ...

Expect only the best end product for your music.

ARRANGEMENTS

In any musician's activity there is a moment where the 'external ear' can help so much in the direction of your musical wishes.

At OPUS MAGNUM, we propose arrangements of your songs to make their impact bigger, from additional instruments to orchestral arrangements, from vocals harmonies to composing ideas.

COACHING

We help you find your literal voice by coaching you before every singing session.
We help you with finger exercises for your guitar, bass or keyboard playing.
We give you advice for better, smoother yet harsher drummings.

You guessed it. We help.

MUSIC THEORY & KNOWLEDGE

Not only can we help you with the sound of it all, but we can also teach you and learn from each other.

Our musical knowledge is always growing and we can offer you many learnings from the history of a music style to 'how it's made' and how to create a specific guitar sound with your gear, which will fit your musical genre, among other things.

THE ENGINEER

(Picture by Emilie Foudelman Photography)

The OPUS MAGNUM STUDIOS is owned and operated by a Belgian individual named Olmo Lipani, nicknamed Déhà for a long, long time, so long it became a second name.

Born in 1985, he discovered music at a very young age and never ever left its side. Always composing and "known" for his outstanding - maybe too big - musical output, he's a passion-driven person, bordering on obsession when it's about music.

Mostly drowned into opposites music styles like Hip-Hop and Extreme Metal, he trained himself with as many instruments as he could put his hands on, but also digitally : from old trackers (OctaMED, Fast Tracker, ...) to modern DAWs (Cubase, ...), he's been making music in every possible styles and started to mix, master it despite his newbie level back then.

With the willpower to do more, Déhà has learnt a lot on his own about music, musicology, theory, mixing, mastering, arrangements and everything offered on this website.

Now with the OPUS MAGNUM STUDIOS unleashed, he can finally showcase more and more of what he was born to do :
Music, in all its forms. And crafting the perfect sound for bands.

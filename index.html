ABOUT

The OPUS MAGNUM STUDIOS gathers musical solutions for your band, project, within any styles of music the world can offer.

We propose a wide array of services - from mixing and mastering to arrangements and music coaching - which are a definite help for your own musical endeavours.

We aim for results which pleases you and only you. We are always considerate with emotional content as a primal factor in our work. We go with the music, for the music.

No big headed sound engineer decision-making, no templates, no compromises.
We craft your desires using our tools, knowledge and professional opinions from scratch.
We create your sound for your band, we help you as you want, how much you want.

You are the master of it all ; are we but willing tools.

OUR WORKS

THE ENGINEER

(Picture by Emilie Foudelman Photography)

The OPUS MAGNUM STUDIO is held by a Belgian individual named Olmo Lipani, nicknamed Déhà for a long, long time it became a second name.

Born in 1985, he discovered music at a very young age and never ever left its side. Always composing and 'known' for his outstanding - maybe too big - musical output, he's a passion-driven person to the corner of obsession when it's about music.

Mostly drowned into opposites music styles like Hip-Hop and Extreme Metal, he trained himself with as many instruments he could put his hands on, but also digitally : from old trackers (OctaMED, Fast Tracker, ...) to modern DAWs (Cubase, ...), he's been making music in every possible styles and started to mix, master it considering his newbie level.

With the willpower to do more, Déhà has learnt a lot on his own about music, musicology, theory, mixing, mastering, arrangements and everything offered on this website.

Now with the OPUS MAGNUM STUDIO unleashed, he can finally showcase more and more of what he was born to do :
Music, in all its forms and crafting the perfect sound for bands.
